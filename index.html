Who is BlueRidge Decon?

Blue Ridge Decon is a family owned and operated business. We primarily offer COVID-19 decontamination for businesses and residential buildings, but also do mold remediation treatments. We offer treatment with a nontoxic, non corrosive, botanical cleaner that is NSF approved for use around food products that we apply with an electrostatic sprayer for complete, even coverage on all surfaces. For more information about us or our treatment process check out our about us page or reach our via phone or email.
